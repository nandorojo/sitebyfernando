PATOS

wearpatos.com

It began as an idea during my freshman year at Penn – a digitally-native footwear brand that would combine minimalist sneakers with traditional Peruvian textiles.

Since our successful Kickstarter campaign in 2016, PATOS has grown into a globally-acclaimed sneaker brand, with thousands of products sold across 15 countries, a partnership with J.Crew, and over a dozen local Peruvian artisans employed.
